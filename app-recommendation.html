How to Get Started

1

Download

Search for 'Stellar Comics' on your app store (Google Play or Apple App Store) and install the app on your device.

2

Browse & Discover

Open the app and explore the extensive library. Use search filters to find specific titles, genres, or discover trending manhua.

3

Read & Enjoy

Tap on any manhua to start reading. Use the download option for offline access and customize your reading experience in settings.

4

Customize

Access reader settings from the menu to adjust brightness, reading direction, page transitions, and other preferences.

5

Engage

Participate in chapter comments, join forum discussions, rate your favorite series, and connect with the community.
